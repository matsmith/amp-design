Let's take the following example. Let's say that we have a root customer named "Cola" which has the following sub companies under it;"Pepsi", "Pepsico", "Pepsiltd", "MT Dew", "Pepsicola."If have have choosen Cola as the customer and searched for "Pepsi" you would get returned all of the previously mentioned root companies display similar to below. Notice that "MT Dew" is not returned. This is because we are only searching on the text that comes after the last dash (-) in the External ID column. This search continues down through EVERY level under the selected customer. Notice that the last entry is returned because "PEPSICO" will match on the work "PEPSI."
